HOW TO PLAY

Use ↑ ↓ ← → arrow keys (or WASD) to steer the snake. On mobile, tap the d-pad above.

Press Space to turn left (−90°) by default. Click the HOTKEY button below the d-pad to rebind it to any key, and use the dropdown to change what it does — turn left, turn right, reverse, or force a direction.

Eat the ■ red food to grow longer and earn points. Don't hit the walls or yourself — game over!

Adjust the speed slider before or during the game. Higher speed = higher challenge (and higher scores).

ABOUT SNAKE

Snake is one of the oldest arcade games, popularized by Nokia phones in the late 1990s. The goal is simple: guide your snake to eat food without crashing. This free browser version requires no download and works on any device.
